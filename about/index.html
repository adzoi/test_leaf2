Leaf 2 Leaf Landscapes was built on one simple belief — that every outdoor space deserves to be extraordinary.

Where It All Began

Leaf 2 Leaf Landscapes was founded over 35 years ago with nothing more than a van, a set of tools, and a passion for transforming outdoor spaces. What started as a one-man operation in Dublin has grown into one of the county's most trusted landscaping and paving companies.

Our founder Mike built this business the old-fashioned way — through hard work, word of mouth, and a refusal to cut corners. Every project we take on, from a modest garden makeover to a full driveway overhaul, gets the same level of attention and care.

Today, Leaf 2 Leaf is a full team of experienced tradespeople, designers, and project managers — but the values haven't changed. We still treat every client's outdoor space as if it were our own.
