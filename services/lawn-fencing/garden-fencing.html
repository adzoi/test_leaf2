Garden Fence Supply & Installation

A good fence defines your boundary, adds privacy and can transform the look of your garden. Whether you need a full run of new fencing or a repair to an existing line, the key is solid posts and correct fixing so the fence stays straight and stable for years.

At Leaf 2 Leaf we supply and install timber and composite panel fencing, horizontal slatted fencing and closeboard. We set posts in concrete at the correct spacing, hang panels or fix rails and boards, and offer repair and replacement of damaged sections.

Installation Time1–2 days (typical residential run)
StylesPanel, slatted, closeboard, composite
PostsConcrete-set for stability
MaterialTimber (treated), composite
Lifespan15–25 years with treated timber and good posts
AlsoRepair, replacement, gate installation
