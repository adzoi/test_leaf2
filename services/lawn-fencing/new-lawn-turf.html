Professional Turf & New Lawn Installation

A new lawn transforms a garden — but only if the ground is prepared properly and the turf is laid and cared for in the first few weeks. Skipping prep or laying turf on poor soil leads to patchy, weedy or failing grass.

At Leaf 2 Leaf we strip or rotavate as needed, level and consolidate the subsoil, add topsoil where required, then lay quality turf in a staggered pattern with joints tight. We advise on watering and aftercare so the lawn establishes quickly and roots down well.

Installation Time1–2 days (laying); usable in 4–6 weeks
TurfQuality Irish turf — hard-wearing, fine or shade mix
IncludesPrep, topsoil if needed, laying, aftercare advice
Establishment2–3 weeks with correct watering
Best timeSpring or autumn — we advise per site

Why Choose Leaf 2 Leaf For New Lawn & Turf?

Correct Laying

Staggered joints, no gaps, rolled and watered — we lay so the turf knits together quickly.

Aftercare Advice

We leave you with clear watering and mowing guidance so the lawn establishes and thrives.
