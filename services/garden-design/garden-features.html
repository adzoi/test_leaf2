Bespoke Garden Features & Focal Points

Garden features — water features, built-in seating, planters, pergolas or fire pits — turn an ordinary garden into a place you want to spend time. They can define zones, create drama or simply give you a spot to sit and enjoy the space.

At Leaf 2 Leaf we design and build bespoke garden features to match your layout and style. We work in stone, brick, timber and metal and can integrate lighting and planting so the feature feels part of the garden, not an afterthought.
