Natural Stone Specialist Installation

Natural stone brings character and longevity to any outdoor space. Each piece is unique in colour and texture, and with the right base and laying technique it will last for decades and only improve with age.

At Leaf 2 Leaf we specialise in Indian sandstone, limestone, granite and slate for patios, paths, steps and walling. We prepare a proper sub-base and mortar bed, lay and point to a high standard, and advise on sealing and aftercare so your investment is protected.

Natural Stone FAQ

Indian sandstone is warm in colour (browns, golds, greys) and often riven for grip. Limestone is typically light grey/cream and can be riven or sawn. Both are durable; choice is mainly aesthetic and budget. We'll show samples.

Sealing is optional but recommended for patios and paths — it reduces staining and moisture absorption and can enhance colour. We advise on products and can arrange sealing as part of the job.

Riven (textured) stone has good grip. Honed or sawn stone can be slippier — we recommend riven for paths and patios in Irish weather, or specify slip-resistant finishes.

With correct sub-base and laying, 25+ years. Granite and slate often outlast sandstone and limestone. Maintenance is minimal — occasional wash and re-seal every few years if sealed.

We'll do our best — bring a photo or we can visit. Natural stone varies by batch so exact match isn't always possible, but we can get close on colour and finish.
